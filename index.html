REBORN COLLISION FIELD
it starts loud — and never asks permission
there is movement even when nothing moves
something inside refuses to stop
not peace — not silence — something between impact and memory
hope, but not soft
hope like pressure in the chest
hope like something still burning under everything
systems collapse but rhythm remains
we don’t organize chaos
we survive inside its structure
this is not branding
this is residue
